Vox
Populi

Vox Populi is our campus newsletter. Released twice every semester, we try to bring everything that happens in our campus to your doorstep. To keep up with times - and also due to cost cuttings - we're going online this year on. But, a newsletter can't function without without writers, reporters and editors.

Editors

An editor will head a section of vox. He/She will edit the articles submitted by the writers and also timely forward them for printing.

Reporters

The reporters will be responsible for interviews and bringing in updates about any events that are going on in the campus.

Writers

The writers will drive Vox. They will be resposible for content generation and paraphrasing the interviews.

Head Web

The head web will be responsible for the maintaining and developing the website.
